<p>The Buona Vista community node is now open!</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/BV_Node_intro_picture.jpg">
</div>
<p>The first community node along the southern stretch of the Rail Corridor,
the 1.6 ha Buona Vista community node located in one-north offers another
recreational and community space in our City in Nature.</p>
<p>Conveniently located between The Metropolis and Elementum (opening soon),
and in close proximity to Buona Vista MRT interchange station, this node
is easily accessible and brings the public closer to nature, heritage and
recreation.</p>
<p>The node is also sensitively integrated with the green spine of the Rail
Corridor and its surrounding developments and features inclusive spaces
for play as well as community and cultural events.</p>
<p><em>How to get to Buona Vista community node:</em>
</p>
<p>By bus: Alight at Opp Blk 43 (74, 91, 95, 100, 105, 106, 111, 147, 185,
191, 196, 198, 200). You can also alight at Buona Vista Station Exit D
(14, 74, 91, 92, 92M, 95, 191, 196, 198, 200, 200A).</p>
<p>By MRT: Alight at Buona Vista MRT Station. Upon exiting the MRT station
(nearest exit is exit D), enter the node via our accesses near The Metropolis.</p>
<p>By car: Parking is available at The Metropolis. Parking charges may apply.</p>
<p><em>Accessibility information:</em> The Buona Vista community node is wheelchair
accessible. From Buona Vista MRT, you can use the lift to reach Exit D
and access Buona Vista Node via our accesses near The Metropolis.</p>

<p>The first vegetated swales along the Rail Corridor have also been planted
within the node, which recall the original stream and marshland landscape
of the Rail Corridor before the railway was first built, and provide new
habitats for dragonflies and other biodiversity to thrive.</p>
